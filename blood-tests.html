Blood tests

I find the following blood tests useful every 9-12 months:

  • Testosterone (free or unbound)
  • HbA1c (diabetes risk)
  • CRP (high sensitivity)
  • Cholesterol (lipid profile)
  • Liver profile
  • Vitamin D

You may already have some or all of these results recently, so it’s always worth asking your GP to share existing results first.

If not, they should be able to arrange them for you – though this can depend on the GP practise, your ability to get across the importance of a proactive approach to healthcare, and/or whether you’ve any existing health conditions.
